sandy.core.lpccov module¶

class sandy.core.lpccov.LpcCov(*args, **kwargs)¶

Bases: sandy.core.basecov.BaseCov

Covariance matrix for energy dependent Legendre polynomials coefficients covariance. Covariances can be stored for:

  • individual polynomial coefficients,

  • cross polynomial coefficients,

  • cross isotopes,
